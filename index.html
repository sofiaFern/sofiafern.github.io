About Me

¡Hola! I’m a recent graduate from University of Maryland with a B.S. in Computer Science and a Minor in Chinese. I’m looking to pursue my interests in tech, business and languages.

I currently work at Splunk as a Software Developer in Test. Working as a Quality Assurance Engineer has taught me the importance of testing new features before launching them to our product and of having clean coding practices.

Other Relevant Experience

hackathons | umdCS | teaching assistant


The moment I stepped on campus in 2016, I joined Technica. Technica is the largest all-women's hackathon in the world and is hosted yearly at the University of Maryland.

Being a part of this community has given me the chance to learn how to be both an efficient and empathetic leader, to build my confidence to stand up for myself and other minorities in tech, and to direct a team of 40+ students and organize a hackathon for over 1000 people.

  • Advisor 2020+
